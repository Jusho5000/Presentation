Section 1

Introduction

The problem, the opportunity, and what LocalFeel solves

Georgia's tourism market generated €4.2 billion in 2025 (Galt & Taggart), with over 55% of inbound tourists coming from Russia, CIS countries, and Israel (GNTA 2025). Tbilisi alone captures ~40% of all visitor traffic. This is a massive, fast-growing market — but the tourist experience is broken.
The core problem is not lack of information — it's information overload. Modern travelers, especially Russian-speaking visitors, face a fragmented landscape: they juggle 3–5 platforms simultaneously (Google Maps, Booking.com, TikTok, Instagram, Telegram groups), spend 3+ hours planning trips, and still can't distinguish authentic recommendations from paid promotions. Our survey of 347 CIS travelers confirms this: 75% use 3+ platforms at once, and 67% spend over 3 hours just deciding where to eat, stay, and visit.
On top of this, 47% face payment issues due to international sanctions — their Russian and Belarusian bank cards are blocked on global platforms like Booking.com and Airbnb. There is no mainstream travel platform that solves this for them.

Problem Analysis: What Survey Data Reveals

Planning Time Spent

Platforms Used Simultaneously

⏱
Problem #1: Planning Takes Too Long
67% spend 3+ hours planning trips, with 34% spending a full day or multiple days. Our AI generates a personalized 3-day plan in under 15 minutes — a 10x improvement. This is validated by survey respondents who rank "scattered information" and "not knowing current prices" as top challenges.
🔎
Problem #2: Information Is Fragmented & Untrusted
75% use 3+ platforms simultaneously. They cross-reference Google Maps, Booking.com, TikTok, Instagram, and Telegram groups — but still can't tell what's authentic. 52% trust only real travelers and friends, not platforms or bloggers. Our verified review system (GPS + booking confirmation) addresses this directly.
💳
Problem #3: Payment Barriers Block Transactions
47% face payment issues due to sanctions (card blocks on international platforms). This rises to 58% for Russians and 62% for Belarusians. No mainstream OTA offers crypto or alternative payment solutions for this audience. We do — and 66% want crypto payments.
💔
Problem #4: Decision Fatigue Leads to Poor Choices
Respondents who used existing platforms report dissatisfaction with choices made under time pressure. They end up at tourist traps because they couldn't research properly. LocalFeel's curation (200+ hand-picked venues) eliminates the "paradox of choice" — every option is pre-vetted.

Section 5

Trust, Bypass Risk & Loyalty Mechanisms

How we earn trust, keep users on-platform, and build retention

Trust is the #1 barrier in travel decisions. Our survey shows: 30% trust real traveler reviews most, 22% trust friend recommendations, and only 7% trust bloggers/influencers. This shapes our entire approach — we build trust through verification, not marketing.

Trust Architecture

Most Trusted Information Sources

✅
Verified Reviews Only
Reviews are allowed only from users who actually visited (confirmed via GPS location + booking record). This eliminates fake reviews entirely — our biggest trust differentiator vs. Google Maps and TripAdvisor.
👁
Every Venue Personally Visited
No scraped data. The team visits and verifies every venue before it enters the database. 200+ Tbilisi venues at launch, each with original Russian-language descriptions and real photos.
🙋
"Local Friend" Framing
We position as a trusted local, not a corporate platform. BMC hypothesis: "Local friend framing beats travel app positioning" — validated by 88% positive reception in survey.
👥
Referral-Based Trust Transfer
19% discover through friends. A recommendation from someone who visited carries more weight than any ad. Our referral program (€2 credit) formalizes this.

Bypass Risk Mitigation

Bypass risk = users discover venues through us but book directly, cutting out our commission. This is the #1 revenue threat for any marketplace. Our strategy:
💰
Price Parity Guarantee
Our 12% commission (vs. OTA 15–25%) means partners offer same or better prices through us. No incentive to bypass.
💳
Payment Lock-In
For sanctioned users, we may be the only way to pay. Crypto/alt payments create natural platform stickiness.
🌟
Loyalty Points
Booking through LocalFeel earns points redeemable for premium itineraries and partner discounts. Bypass = lose rewards.

Loyalty & Retention

Georgia has a ~20% repeat visit rate (GNTA). Our target is to increase this to 30% through post-trip engagement:
📨
Post-Trip Push: "Share Your Hidden Gem"
After trip, prompt users to leave verified reviews and share UGC. Creates content flywheel. Sharing generates referral codes for friends.
🔁
Pre-Trip for Repeat Visitors
"Welcome back! Here's what's new in Tbilisi since your last visit." Personalized updates based on past preferences and new venue additions.
KPI targets: Bypass rate <25% (Phase 1) → <15% (Phase 2) → <10% (Year 2). NPS target: 40 → 50 → 60. Partner retention: 75% → 85% → 90%.
